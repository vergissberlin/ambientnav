Two LED strips work together to guide you on the road: the front strip reacts to navigation, the rear strip helps you reverse safely.

<LedEffectsVisualizer />

## Front LEDs

The front strip responds to your current driving situation:

- **Ambient Glow** — no active navigation, a calm breathing light fills the strip.
- **Turn Left / Turn Right** — a light sweeps toward the turn direction when your next turn is less than 200 m away.
- **Go Straight** — a soft pulse appears in the center of the strip.
- **Left / Right Indicator** — the corresponding half of the strip blinks while your indicator is on.
- **Hazard** — the entire strip blinks when hazard lights are active.

## Rear LEDs

Engage reverse gear and the rear strip automatically switches to parking mode:

- Three zones — **left**, **center**, **right** — each correspond to one distance sensor at the bumper.
- A full bright bar means the path is clear.
- The bar shortens and shifts color as an obstacle gets closer.
- A short, rapidly blinking bar means **stop** — an obstacle is very close.

Shift back into any forward gear and the rear strip returns to its ambient glow.

## Quick Setup

### 1. Install the App

Download **AmbientNav** from the App Store and open it on your iPhone.

### 2. Flash the Firmware

Follow the [Firmware Guide](/flash/) to load the software onto both microcontrollers — no coding required.

### 3. Connect the Hardware

The [Wiring Guide](/wiring/) walks you through connecting the LED strips, distance sensors, and power supply step by step.

### 4. Power On and Connect

Connect the power supply, open the app, and tap **Connect** to pair with your vehicle. The lights come on automatically.
